Sodium grease

  • Sodium grease made from the finest raw materials and industrial oils
  • High protest to withstand push-button operation, mechanical and thermal loads and its shape

Thermal Grease

  • A high-performance grease characterized by its high ability to operate at high temperatures and extreme mechanical loads
  • Excellent corrosion and rust resistance
  • High heat resistance up to 250 degrees Celsius
  • Suitable for Pullman bearing lubrication, cable lubrication, oil seal and various mechanical applications

Grease 118

  • Calcium grease made from the finest raw materials and industrial oils
  • High ability to withstand harsh working conditions
  • Superior protection from rust and corrosion for non-metallic parts
  • It is used to lubricate chassis, axles and metal surfaces
  • Suitable for agricultural tractors, irrigation machines and heavy transport vehicles
